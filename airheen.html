Design Philosophy

I am Airheen, and this passion has been with me since I was little. Even as a child, I found immense joy in decorating our house and playing games that involved designing interiors and creating different spaces.

The process of arranging furniture, selecting colors, and transforming a blank space into something beautiful and functional truly excites me. It’s more than just a hobby; it's a way for me to express creativity and influence the mood and comfort of a room.

As a Grade 9 student, I see this project as a perfect opportunity to explore and deepen my interest in this field. I am fascinated by how good design can impact people's lives, and I am eager to learn what it takes to create beautiful, inspiring, and harmonious environments for others.
